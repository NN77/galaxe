SECURITY DEPOSIT REQUIRED

When collecting the spaceship, you will be required to leave a deposit of € 100,00 that will be retained for all the rental duration. The deposit will be released within 24 hours after the rental end. The amount will be available again according to the technical credit card processes and policies.

CREDIT CARD POLICY

When collecting the spaceship, a credit card will be required to secure the deposit for guarantee. Please, make sure that the availability is enough to cover the required amount. The credit card must be traditional: ATM and debit cards won't be accepted. Also, the credit card must be entitled to the renter. To rent some special spaceship groups (premium and GXEFAST) an additional credit card will be required, running on a different circuit. When required, the additional credit card shall run on one of the following circuits: Visa Gold, American Express or Diners. Please, contact our Call Center for more information.

PERSONAL DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

When collecting the spaceship, please don't forget to bring with you your ID card (or your passport if you are not UE citizen) complete with your home address and your pilot's license, that will have to be valid since a minimum of 1 year. Please remember that the pilot's license must be valid for international use, otherwise it will have to be accompanied by a translation made from an embassy or an equivalent authority, recognized by the Universe authorities.
